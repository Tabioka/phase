Phase is a free to use, open source Discord bot built by
[mikaeladev](https://phasebot.xyz/redirect/developer). It aims to be the
all-in-one solution for as many servers as possible, with a large variety of
different modules and commands.

Whether you're seeking to enhance a small server shared with friends or
overseeing a bustling community, Phase is your solution for effectively
managing, moderating, and improving your Discord servers.
